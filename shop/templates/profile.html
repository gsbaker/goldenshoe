how do I return my order?

If you've taken your new shoes for their 30-day trial run and you've decided—for any reason— that they're just not perfect, here's how to get started on a return.

On your orders page, scroll to one of the items you'd like to return and tap "View or Manage" to open the order. Then within the order, select "Start Return".

how long will my order take to ship?

Orders usually arrive within 1-2 Business Days. We use standard UK shipping on all orders.
